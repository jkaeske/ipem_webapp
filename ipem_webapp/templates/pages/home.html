The integrated Product engineering Model (iPeM)

The iPeM is a generic meta-model which comprises the relevant elements to derive situation-specific product development process models. The iPeM is based on the system triple of product engineering: it describes product engineering as a continuous interaction of the system of objectives, the system of objects and the operation system.

  • Are there also multiple generations for the layers: Strategy, Validation-System and Production-System?
    Yes there can be! The typical picture shown above only shows the two product generations: Gn and Gn+1. But, depending on the product development process, there can be more generations for each and every layer of the meta-model. For example: Strategy Gn, Strategy Gn-1 and/or Production-System Gn-1.
